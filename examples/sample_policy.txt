ACME CORP - INTERNAL POLICIES HANDBOOK
Version 3.2 | Effective: January 2025

SECTION 4: EXPENSE AND PROCUREMENT POLICY
4.1 All expenses above $500 require prior manager approval.
4.2 Expenses above $5,000 require VP-level approval and a purchase order.
4.3 Employee reimbursements must be submitted within 30 days of the expense.
4.4 Corporate credit cards may not be used for personal expenses.
4.5 All vendor contracts above $10,000 must go through the procurement review process.

SECTION 7: DATA HANDLING AND PRIVACY POLICY
7.1 Customer PII must be encrypted at rest and in transit (AES-256 minimum).
7.2 Access to customer data requires role-based authorization and is logged.
7.3 Customer data retention shall not exceed 24 months unless legally required.
7.4 Data exports containing PII must be approved by the Data Privacy Officer.
7.5 All employees must complete annual data privacy training.

SECTION 9: IT SECURITY POLICY
9.1 Passwords must be at least 12 characters with complexity requirements.
9.2 Multi-factor authentication (MFA) is required for all systems.
9.3 Software installations require IT department approval.
9.4 Security patches must be applied within 14 days of release.
9.5 USB storage devices are prohibited on company networks.

SECTION 11: HR AND EMPLOYMENT POLICY
11.1 All hiring decisions must include at least two interview rounds.
11.2 Background checks are mandatory for all new hires.
11.3 Employee performance reviews must be conducted annually.
11.4 Overtime must be pre-approved by the department manager.
11.5 All workplace incidents must be reported within 24 hours.
