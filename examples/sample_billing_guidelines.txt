LEGAL BILLING GUIDELINES - Effective January 2025

1. TIME ENTRY REQUIREMENTS
   1.1 All time entries must include a specific description of work performed.
   1.2 Vague descriptions such as "research," "phone call," "emails," "worked on matter,"
       "various tasks," "admin," or "stuff" are NOT acceptable.
   1.3 Each entry must identify the specific task, document, or issue addressed.
   1.4 Minimum billable increment: 0.25 hours (15 minutes).
   1.5 Block billing (combining multiple tasks into one entry) is discouraged.
       Separate entries should be made for distinct tasks.

2. DAILY HOUR LIMITS
   2.1 No single attorney may bill more than 10.0 hours per day to a single client.
   2.2 Total daily billing across all clients should not exceed 12.0 hours.
   2.3 Entries exceeding these limits require pre-approval and written justification.

3. RATE GUIDELINES
   3.1 Partner rate: $400-$600/hour
   3.2 Senior Associate rate: $300-$450/hour
   3.3 Junior Associate rate: $200-$350/hour
   3.4 Paralegal/Support rate: $150-$300/hour
   3.5 Rate increases require 30-day written notice.

4. PROHIBITED CHARGES
   4.1 Administrative tasks (filing, scheduling, copying) may not be billed at attorney rates.
   4.2 Internal meetings exceeding 1.0 hours require justification.
   4.3 Travel time is billed at 50% of the standard rate.
   4.4 Training or professional development time is not billable.

5. REVIEW AND APPROVAL
   5.1 All entries flagged by compliance review must be revised within 5 business days.
   5.2 Repeated violations may result in write-offs or rate adjustments.
   5.3 Entries under 0.2 hours for tasks like "reviewed email" should be aggregated.
