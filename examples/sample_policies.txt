=== HR POLICY (Revised Jan 2024) ===

HR-1: Remote Work Policy
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week with manager approval.
Remote employees must be available during core hours (9 AM - 3 PM local time).
All remote work arrangements must be documented and reviewed quarterly.

HR-2: Data Access for Employees
All employees are granted access to company systems based on their role.
Access reviews are conducted annually by HR.
Terminated employees must have access revoked within 48 hours.

HR-3: Employee Monitoring
The company reserves the right to monitor all communications on company devices.
Employee personal devices used for work are not subject to monitoring.
All monitoring must comply with local privacy laws.

HR-4: Time-Off Policy
Employees are entitled to 15 days PTO per year, accruing monthly.
Unused PTO may be carried over up to 5 days into the next calendar year.
PTO requests require 2 weeks advance notice for periods over 3 days.

=== IT SECURITY POLICY (Revised Mar 2023) ===

IT-1: Access Control
All system access requires multi-factor authentication.
Access is reviewed semi-annually by IT Security.
Terminated employees must have access revoked within 24 hours.

IT-2: Device Management
Only company-issued devices may connect to the corporate network.
Personal devices may connect to the guest WiFi network only.
All company devices must have endpoint protection software installed.

IT-3: Data Classification
Data is classified as: Public, Internal, Confidential, Restricted.
Confidential and Restricted data must be encrypted at rest.
Internal data does not require encryption.
All customer data is classified as Restricted.

IT-4: Incident Response
Security incidents must be reported within 1 hour of discovery.
The CISO must be notified of all Critical and High severity incidents.
Incident post-mortems are required within 5 business days.

=== DATA PRIVACY POLICY (Revised Jun 2022) ===

DP-1: Data Collection
Personal data may only be collected with explicit user consent.
Data collection must be limited to what is necessary for the stated purpose.
Consent records must be maintained for 3 years.

DP-2: Data Retention
Customer data must be retained for a minimum of 5 years for regulatory purposes.
Personal data must be deleted within 30 days of a deletion request (GDPR Right to Erasure).
Backup data is exempt from deletion requirements.

DP-3: Data Sharing
Customer data may not be shared with third parties without written consent.
Data sharing agreements must be reviewed by Legal before execution.
Anonymized data may be shared freely for research purposes.

DP-4: Breach Notification
Data breaches must be reported to affected individuals within 72 hours.
The DPO must be notified immediately upon discovery of any breach.
Breaches affecting fewer than 100 records do not require individual notification.

=== COMPLIANCE POLICY (Revised Nov 2023) ===

CP-1: Regulatory Training
All employees must complete compliance training within 30 days of hire.
Annual refresher training is mandatory for all employees.
Training completion rates must exceed 95% company-wide.

CP-2: Whistleblower Protection
Employees may report compliance concerns anonymously through the ethics hotline.
Retaliation against whistleblowers is strictly prohibited.
All reports must be investigated within 14 business days.

CP-3: Record Retention
Financial records must be retained for 7 years (SOX requirement).
HR records must be retained for 5 years after employment ends.
All records retention follows the longest applicable requirement.
